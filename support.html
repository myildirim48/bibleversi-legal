Frequently asked

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the paywall (gold crown on the Today tab) and tap Restore purchases.
How do I cancel my subscription?
iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Bible - Daily Verse, Prayer → Cancel. Your access continues until the period ends. We also remind you 2 days before your free trial converts — no surprise charges, ever.
Notifications or the alarm aren't arriving?
Check iPhone Settings → Notifications → Bible and make sure notifications are allowed. The "Wake up to a verse" full alarm requires iOS 26; on older versions it arrives as a time-sensitive notification.
How do I add the widget?
Long-press your home or lock screen → tap + / Customize → search "Bible".
How do I use a painting as my wallpaper?
In the app: palette button → long-press any painting → Save wallpaper to Photos, then set it from your Photos app.
